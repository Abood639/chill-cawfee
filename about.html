Our Story

Fishhawk's Family-Run Living Room

We started Chill Cawfee in 2020 to bring neighbors together. Today, mother and son Lisa and Hunter serve up rich custom drinks and a warm physical space for real connection.

Come Visit Us
The Timeline

From Valrico to Fishhawk

Our dream began at the start of Covid. We realized people needed physical connection more than ever, leading us to establish a true neighborhood third place.

2020

The Dream Begins

Lisa and Tom founded Chill Cawfee in Valrico during a time of isolation, determined to create a comforting sanctuary where neighbors could share a table and a warm handshake.

Present Day

Our New Home

Now operated by Lisa and Tom's son Hunter, we relocated to Fishhawk to serve our home community. We brought our signature sweet drinks and lively event calendar with us.

Behind the Bar

Lisa and Hunter

Lisa brings the welcoming warmth that turns first-time visitors into daily regulars, hosting book clubs and networking groups. Her son Hunter handles the bar, pulling rich espresso shots and crafting our signature sweet dreams.

Together, they keep the shop running as a lively, unpretentious living room. Whether you are here for an entrepreneurial mastermind or a quiet afternoon escape, they always make sure there is an open chair for you.
